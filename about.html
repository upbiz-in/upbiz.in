About Me

Hi, I’m Yogaraj, a student and the founder of Upbiz, a digital services agency dedicated to helping small businesses and individuals establish a strong online presence. While managing my studies, I’ve also gained hands-on experience helping businesses grow through digital solutions. With Upbiz, I’ve worked on creating websites, managing online presence, and supporting businesses to showcase their work effectively. At Upbiz, I believe in transparency, quality, and results. I’m passionate about learning, problem-solving, and building practical solutions that make a real impact. Balancing education and entrepreneurship has taught me discipline, creativity, and how to turn ideas into action.

How I Got Here

I started out as a student curious about technology and business, learning how websites and digital tools could help companies grow. Along the way, I realized I could combine my skills and passion to actually help businesses thrive online. Each project I took on, from building websites to managing social media for small businesses, helped me gain real-world experience and confidence. That’s how Upbiz was born

Why Work With Me

I understand what it’s like to juggle multiple responsibilities because I’m a student and an entrepreneur at the same time. This balance has taught me discipline, creativity, and the importance of delivering results.

By working with me, you get someone who: - Brings hands-on experience helping businesses grow online. - Offers practical, personalized solutions for websites, social media, and digital presence. - Values transparency, quality, and timely results. - Is passionate about learning, problem-solving, and making a real impact. I’m committed to helping your business succeed while keeping the process simple and effective.
